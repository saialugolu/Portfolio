The Journey So Far

Understanding customers has always been at the center of my career.

My career started in Customer Success, where I learned that understanding users goes far beyond resolving support requests. It is about understanding their goals, identifying the actual problem behind a question, setting clear expectations, and helping customers achieve meaningful outcomes.

Over time, I became increasingly interested in how products are designed, why users behave in certain ways, how features are researched and built, and how the right product can be taken to the right audience.

Today, my experience spans Customer Success, product work, product analytics, user research, PRDs, user stories, SEO and AEO content strategy, LinkedIn advertising, B2B outreach, ICP development, lead generation, and product-led growth experimentation. As a Growth Product Manager, I enjoy connecting customer insights with product strategy, collaborating across teams, validating ideas through experimentation, and helping products reach the right users in the right way.
